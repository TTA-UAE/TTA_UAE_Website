How to Pack a Package in a safe way?

Before your package arrives at the designated address, the shipment may pass through various modes of transportation and sorting facilities. The greater the distance between the sender and the recipient, the more stages the package transports. Along with these, there is a potential risk of damage to the package - especially if you have secured it insufficiently.

How do I Package custom shipments?

If you plan to send by courier an irregularly shaped item or dimensions that exceed 120 x 60 x 60 cm - send it on TTA as a non-standard package. This is an additional paid service that also allows for the shipment of items in loose packaging, glassware and liquid substances. Remember, however, that a non-standard shipment requires proper protection during transport.
